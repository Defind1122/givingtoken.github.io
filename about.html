What is Giving Token?

Giving Token is a decentralized application with ethereum smart contract or more popularly called erc20.

Giving Token has been supported by popular wallets on your computer, your smartphone, or websites like Mew, Metamask, Trustwallet, Imtoken and other erc20 wallets.

One of the most popular uses for Giving Token is "tipping" fellow internet-goers who create or share great content. Think of it as a more meaningful "like" or upvote, with real value that can be used all across the internet.
